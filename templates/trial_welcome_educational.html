Turn Stories Into Learning Moments

1

Ask Open-Ended Questions

"What do you think will happen next?" "How would you solve this problem?" These questions develop critical thinking.

2

Connect to Real Life

"Remember when you were brave like that?" Relate story events to your child's experiences.

3

Extend the Learning

Draw pictures, act out scenes, or create crafts based on the story. Multi-sensory learning sticks!

4

Celebrate New Words

Point out new vocabulary and use it throughout the day. "That was courageous, just like in your story!"
