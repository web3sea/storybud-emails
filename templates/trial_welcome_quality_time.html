Making Story Time Extra Special

Create a Cozy Reading Nook: Use pillows, blankets, and soft lighting to make a special story corner.

Use Character Voices: Give each character a unique voice - kids love when Grandma sounds like Grandma!

Ask Questions: "What would you do?" "How do you think they felt?" Turn reading into conversation.

No Devices Rule: Make story time a phone-free zone for both of you. This is your special time together.
